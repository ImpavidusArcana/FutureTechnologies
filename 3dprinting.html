3D Printing:

3D Printing is the process of creating or manufacturing complex three dimensional objects.

3D Printing and Art:

3D printing is often used to create detailed art pieces that are almost impossible to create by hand.

3D Printing and Housing:

In today's world, with housing being as expensive as it is, 3D printing can now be used to create houses faster and at a relatively lower price depending upon the materials that are used.

3D Printing uses in Medicine:

3D printing is used in medicine by means of creating prostethics, anatomical models used for educational purposes or surgical planning, implants such as dental, spinal or hip, and bioprinting which will one day create something as complex as skin for burn victims.

3D Printing and the Automotive Industry:

In the automotive industry, 3D printing is used to create car parts such as door panel repair kits, brake cooling ducts, pedal spacers, and cupholders.
