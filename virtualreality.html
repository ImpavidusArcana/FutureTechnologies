Virtual Reality:

Definition:

Virtual Reality or VR is an immersive 3-D environment that is computer generated. This makes the user feel as if the objects and overall environment to be real.

VR and Video Games:

Video games that offer virtual reality typically involve in using devices such as a meta quest, for example, that is placed on a users head. This device will provide a small screen that scales the room the user is in and also tracks the movement of the user within the space that is used, creating a very immersive environment.

VR and Healthcare:

VR has been used to teach up and coming heatlhcare professionals by offering a safe environment for future professionals to work on virtual patients and not to run the risk of harming real patients. Other uses in healthcare, include therapy for individuals that may struggle with their mental health. These types of therapy may include practicing new skills, overcoming a fear that is set in a safe environment, or even to help with social interactions.
